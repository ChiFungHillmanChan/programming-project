Taiwan History

In 1895, during the Japanese rule of Taiwan, Taiwanese culture began to shift from native to contemporary global culture due to its location on East Asian trade routes. Taiwan's elites learned Japanese culture and language without interfering with their religion in an effort to Japaneseize the island nation. Under the influence of American culture, the Chinese Nationalist Party (KMT) realigned Taiwan from Japanese imperialism to Chinese nationalism.

Kenting National Park, Taiwan

Bathed in beautiful sunshine, Kenting is marvelous to explore and there is loads of fauna and flora on show. One of the must-see attractions is the huge Eluanbi Lighthouse, which towers over its surroundings. A fabulous national park to visit, Kenting also hosts the famous Spring Scream festival which attracts thousands of music lovers every year.

Taipei 101, Taiwan

The capital of Taiwan, Taipei, is an amazing place with lots to see and do for foreigners. Not only the political of the island, but also the cultural center because of the living arts and cultural scene. There is a iconic building representing Taipei, which is Taipei 101. Actually Taipei 101 was once the tallest building in the world. On top of the building, you can see clearly about the whole view of this fascinating city.

Shilin night market, Taiwan

Food in night market, Taiwan

Shilin Night Market is one of the most famous and largest night markets in Taipei. It wakes uo in the early evening and fills up quickly especially on holidays. Not just interest by foreigners but also local coming to eat, browse and find bargains, souvenirs. The market follows the route to the Palace Museum, making it the next stop for museum visitors. Contrary to the misleading name, the night market is located across the street from Jiantan Train Station, not the continuous Shilin Train Station.

Taiwan Culture

Families and ancestors

wishing sign, Taiwan

Most Taiwanese family has a very close relationship with their parents even after married. This surprises westerners because of the interference, but that Taiwanese parents do: offer level of supports to their children even they are adult. It is not a news for parents to buy a house for their child of choose the subject for them to study. Also, it is still common for three or more generations to live in one. Parents also hope that the eldest son will live with his wife in the same house with parents.

Taiwan event: Pingxi Sky Lantern Festival

Pingxi Sky Lantern Festival, the most famous festivals in Taiwan that attracts thousands of people every year. During the Lantern Festival, people go to Pingxi Village to have a sky lanterns into the night sky. Other than that, they will write the wishes on the Lantern. There was a saying the wishes on the Lantern will become true in future.
