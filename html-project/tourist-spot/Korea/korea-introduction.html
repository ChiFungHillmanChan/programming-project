Korea History

Korea is a mountainous peninsula surronded by the sea on three sides. The Korean peninsula measures about 600 miles from its northern tip to its southern end. It shares its northern borders with China and Russia, and its closest southern neighbor is Japan.

Korea Kimchi

Kimnchi is the sliced cabbage fermented with some red pepper paste and anachovy paste. Koreans love it and enjoy it with every meal as side dish. Kimchi is a symbol of Korean culture because it is unique in smelling and tasting. Some foreigners cannot stand with it because of it's spicy and sour taste. However people who love Kimchi will earn the heartfely respecty of the locals. This is a must to get this food experiences in Korea.
